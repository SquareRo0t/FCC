Classic Homemade Apple Pie Recipe

A classic, homemade apple pie is a versatile and popular choice. Here is a recipe for a traditional double-crust apple pie that combines a flaky, buttery crust with a perfectly spiced, tender apple filling.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 6–8 tablespoons ice water

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Crust: In a food processor, pulse flour, salt, and sugar. Add cold butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs with some pea-sized pieces. Add ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, pulsing until the dough just holds together. Divide dough in half, form into discs, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Preheat and Prepare: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
  3. Make the Filling: In a large bowl, toss the sliced apples with lemon juice, sugars, flour, cinnamon, and nutmeg until evenly coated.
  4. Assemble the Pie: On a floured surface, roll out one disc of dough into a 12-inch circle. Place it into a 9-inch pie plate. Pour in the apple filling, mounding it slightly in the center, and dot with 2 tablespoons of butter pieces.
